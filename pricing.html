Pricing

Simple plans for focused watching.

PlayMySubs starts with a usable free layer and upgrades into a queue-control tool for people who follow subscriptions daily.

Free

Core playback

For watching subscribed channels in a floating player with manual control.

  • Floating mini player overlay
  • Latest and Live tabs
  • Play / pause / next / previous
  • Volume control
  • Subscription scraping without YouTube API keys

Premium

Queue control

For users who want a cleaner subscription queue and less manual skipping.

  • Favorite channels with priority order
  • Hide channels from playback
  • Priority Start when a favorite has a playable video
  • Surf Mode auto-next
  • Shuffle option inside the player
  • License-based activation inside the extension

Purchase flow

When checkout is live, Premium purchases will deliver a license key by email. That key is pasted into the extension to unlock Premium features. Final public pricing will appear directly on checkout and this page before launch.
