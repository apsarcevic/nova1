Pricing

Simple plans for focused watching.

PlayMySubs starts with a usable free layer and upgrades into a queue-control tool for people who follow subscriptions daily. Premium is currently positioned as a simple one-time purchase.

Free

Core playback

$0 forever

For watching subscribed channels in a floating player with manual control.

  • Floating mini player overlay
  • Latest and Live tabs
  • Play / pause / next / previous
  • Volume control
  • Subscription scraping without YouTube API keys

Premium

Queue control

$5 one-time

For users who want a cleaner subscription queue and less manual skipping without a recurring subscription.

  • Favorite channels with priority order
  • Hide channels from playback
  • Priority Start when a favorite has a playable video
  • Surf Mode auto-next
  • Shuffle option inside the player
  • License-based activation inside the extension

Purchase flow

Premium is currently priced at $5 as a one-time purchase. After checkout, the customer receives a license key by email and pastes it into the extension to unlock Premium features.
