How it works

Built around a simple loop: sync, watch, unlock better control.

Step 1

Open the player

The extension loads your subscription feed and gives you a floating playback layer instead of another full watch page.

Step 2

Watch Live or Latest

Browse subscribed channels through the mini player, switch tabs fast, and keep YouTube in the background instead of in control.

Step 3

Unlock Premium

After checkout, a license key unlocks favorites, hidden channels, priority start, and auto-next behavior in the extension.
