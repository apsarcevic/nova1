About

Built for focused watching.

PlayMySubs is designed for people who want a cleaner way to follow YouTube subscriptions without opening a full watch page every time.

Our goal is simple: less friction, faster playback, and a more reliable daily queue for people who actually use subscriptions as a habit.

Product direction

  • Free should stay useful for core playback.
  • Premium should earn its place through better queue control, not artificial restrictions.
  • The extension should remain lightweight, stable, and understandable.
