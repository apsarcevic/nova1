Privacy

Privacy policy.

Effective date: March 19, 2026

We collect only the information required to sell, activate, support, and maintain PlayMySubs.

What we collect

  • Your email address when you purchase Premium or contact support.
  • License-related metadata needed to activate and verify Premium access.
  • Operational diagnostics that are reasonably necessary to maintain service stability, prevent abuse, or troubleshoot activation problems.

What the extension stores locally

  • Playback preferences such as selected tab, queue position, favorite channels, hidden channels, and player layout settings.
  • Your license status and activation metadata needed to determine whether Premium features are unlocked.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not ask for your YouTube password.
  • We do not claim ownership of YouTube content you watch through the extension.

Third-party services

  • Payments and merchant-of-record services may be handled by Paddle or another payment provider shown during checkout.
  • Email, hosting, analytics, and database infrastructure may be handled by third-party providers as reasonably necessary to operate the service.
  • Those providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

Data retention

  • We keep customer, license, and support records for as long as reasonably necessary to deliver access, handle refunds, prevent abuse, and maintain business records.
  • Local extension preferences remain in your browser storage unless you remove them or uninstall the extension.

Contact

If you need privacy-related support, contact hello@playmysubs.com.
