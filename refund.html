Refund

Refund policy.

If your purchase does not work as expected, contact support within 14 days of purchase.

When refunds are typically approved

  • The license was delivered but activation does not work and support could not resolve the issue in a reasonable time.
  • You were billed incorrectly.
  • The product materially failed to match the described core functionality at the time of purchase.

When refunds may be denied

  • The request is abusive or fraudulent.
  • The issue was caused by misuse, unsupported environments, or license sharing.
  • The request arrives outside the stated refund window unless a legal right requires otherwise.

We will investigate quickly and issue a refund when eligibility is confirmed.
