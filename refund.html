Refund

Refund policy.

Effective date: March 19, 2026

PlayMySubs offers a 14-day refund window for eligible purchases. Refund handling is managed in line with Paddle’s buyer and merchant-of-record processes.

Refund window

  • Customers may request a refund within 14 days of the original purchase date.
  • Where Paddle is the merchant of record, refunds are processed through Paddle’s systems and review flow.
  • Mandatory consumer rights that apply in your jurisdiction remain unaffected.

How to request a refund

  • Use the link in your Paddle receipt or contact hello@playmysubs.com and include the email address used for checkout.
  • Please include any relevant order details and a short explanation of the issue so the request can be matched to the transaction.
  • Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method according to Paddle’s processing timelines.

Delivery issues

  • If you do not receive your license key or cannot activate Premium, contact us first at hello@playmysubs.com so we can try to resolve the issue quickly.
  • If the issue is not resolved, you may still request a refund within the refund window.

We aim to handle refund and delivery-related support promptly and in good faith.
